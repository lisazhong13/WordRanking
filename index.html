Background and Motivation

As a university student who is about to graduate, I have a mixed feeling of excitement and anxiety swirling inside me. On one hand, I'm thrilled to finally step into the next stage of my life—starting a career or moving on to further research. On the other hand, there's a nagging fear: Will my university's reputation and global ranking help me stand out in the job market or academic circles?

What are we trying to do

By uncovering these insights, we aim to provide a deeper understanding of the evolving landscape of global higher education and the factors that drive institutional success. Our goal is to create a tool that helps stakeholders—such as students, educators, policymakers, and institutions—make informed decisions by visualizing trends, identifying areas of improvement, and fostering collaboration in the pursuit of academic excellence.
